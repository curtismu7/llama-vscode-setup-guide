Audience: sales leaders, AEs, and SEs
What changes — and what does not

What changes

There is now a clear path to bring in deeper AI architecture support for qualified opportunities, workshops, and POCs.

What stays the same

The AE and POD SE still own the deal. The specialist is added to strengthen the team, not to replace it.

How work gets routed

Requests move through a formal intake and review path so capacity can be prioritized instead of handled ad hoc.

How the model scales

Specialists remain in their regions while the program captures reusable patterns, field feedback, and enablement learnings.

This keeps the operating model simple: focused specialist depth where it matters, with no new overlay organization to explain.
